  • Dinosaurus

    Prehistoric RTS Game

    Dinosaurus was a project I made with a friend, on Unity 3D. I was in charge of Art style and Game Design. The game was a RTS (Real Time Strategy) game set on a prehistoric era where dinosaurs walked together with humans, just like in the Flintstones. Five clans was in the game, with each different victory conditions. For one, annihilation on the others groups, for another, having enough mpney to build a statue of himself, and for last, having more than 80% of the NPC under your command. The game was a mixed of Warcraft for the gameplay, and Don't Stave for the art style. It went under due to time and money managment, and lack of motivation from both parts.

  • 20 000 Lieues Sans La Mer

    Puzzle/Platformer

    20 000 Lieues Sans La Mer (20.000 Leagues Witout The Sea) was a student project made by twenty students including myself, made during the course of Level/Game Designer run by Gamagora. You, as the mighty god of the Sea Poseidon, and also a sponge, are tasked to understand why the sea disapeared over night, and bring back the order under the sea. Made within three months, the game was a 9 levels short game, representing a third of the game if it was produced further. I was in charge of a bit of Game design and Art design. They were tension over the team, so the prject was able to be what we though it would be. Nonetheless, I think it did a good job to demonstrate what you were capable of.
